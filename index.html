Mic line

These are essentially the cable or connection that links a microphone to audio recording or sound reinforcement equipment. It plays a crucial role in transmitting the microphone's audio signal accurately and is an essential part of the audio recording and reinforcement process. Mic Stand

A microphone stand is a piece of equipment used to support and position a microphone during various audio recording, broadcasting, or live performance applications. Available mics, Tripod Stand, Boom Stand, Desk Stand (Desktop Stand), Floor Stand, Overhead Stand, Adjustable Height, Boom Arm, Microphone Clip or Holder, Base, Portability, Thread Size, Shock Mounts and Cable Management..

Head mic

A "head microphone," often referred to as a "headset microphone" or simply a "headset mic," is a type of microphone that is worn on the head, typically as part of a headset. These microphones are designed to capture the audio directly from the wearer's mouth while leaving their hands free for other tasks or activities. Available mics, Headset Microphone, Lavalier Microphone (Lapel Mic), Earset Microphone (Earworn Microphone), Bone Conduction Microphone, Throat Microphone, Helmet Microphone, Virtual Reality (VR) Microphone..
